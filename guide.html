Low Intensity Support

  • Practice mindful breathing for 5 minutes
  • Take a short walk in nature
  • Write down three things you're grateful for
  • Listen to calming music
  • Do some light stretching

Moderate Intensity Support

  • Practice progressive muscle relaxation
  • Call a friend or family member
  • Try journaling your thoughts and feelings
  • Do some physical exercise
  • Use grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1 method)

High Intensity Support

  • Contact a mental health professional
  • Use crisis helpline services
  • Practice immediate grounding exercises
  • Remove yourself from triggering situations
  • Use emergency contacts from your safety plan
